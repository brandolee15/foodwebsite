How to make Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients list

Pizza Sauce

  • 30 grams of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Pizza Dough

  • 550 grams of all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 60 grams of yeast
  • 300 millilitres of water
  • 70 millilitres of olive oil

Toppings

  • 1 package of pepperoni
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese (lots)

Equipment list

  • Teaspoon
  • Tablespoon
  • Rolling Pin
  • Measuring jug
  • Sieve
  • Mixing bowl
  • Pizza pan
  • Oven
  • Spatula

Directions

  1. For sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with 120 millilitres of water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust. Freeze remaining paste.
  2. For dough: Combine 300 grams of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 minute). Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.
  3. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed. (If using RapidRiseÂ® Yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)
  4. Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.
  5. For pizzas: Preheat oven to 220 degrees Celcius. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and cheese.
  6. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
